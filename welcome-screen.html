Welcome to Atti

Please follow these rules:

Be a Digital Superhero, Not a Troll
Unleash your inner superhero! Treat everyone with respect, as if they’re your sidekick. Harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse won't be tolerated. Keep it friendly – no offensive memes, personal attacks, or threats. Everyone deserves a positive and welcoming experience.

Truth Serum Activated
Stay true to your word! No fake news, impersonation, or misleading content. Honesty is the foundation of a trustworthy community. Keep the fiction in the comic books, and maintain transparency in all your interactions.

Every Village Has Its Own Rules
Our universe is vast and diverse. Each community or group has its own set of rules. Whether you're in the 'Puppies of the Galaxy' group or the 'Martian Chefs' forum, follow the specific guidelines and respect the local community vibe.

Zero Tolerance for Objectionable Content
We have a strict zero-tolerance policy on any content that promotes hate, violence, discrimination, or illegal activities. This includes, but is not limited to, hate speech, explicit content, threats, harassment, and profanity. Violation of this policy will result in immediate action, including account suspension or removal.

Be a Hero, Report the Zero
If you see something harmful, offensive, or against the rules, don't hesitate – flag it. By reporting objectionable content, you help us keep the community safe and enjoyable for all users.
